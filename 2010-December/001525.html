Hi Dave,

The browsers for other modes is a very exciting development!

I suggest that you could identify modes based on spectrogram footprint 
and/or RSID, and have a browser that would demodulate each signal in the 
passband, even if they were mixed modes present.

It would also be interesting to change the sample rate to something 
larger so that more signals could be demodulated.  I think we have 
enough CPU to do this now.  I hacked that once a year or more ago, and 
it did work, but it would be nice to have it as an option for users to 
pick.

I plan to set up several more copies of fldigi running on our websdr 
server, to tune other frequencies and modes than the two PSK31 "bands" I 
have presently.  You can listen to the websdr here 
<http://outside.wallawalla.edu:8901/>, and see its psk spots here 
<http://psk.gladstonefamily.net/pskmap.html?preset&callsign=K7UEB&txrx=rx&timerange=86400&hidepink=1>.  
It is using your alpha code.
