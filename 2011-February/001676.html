Actually, LoTW will ignore exact dupes. If you load your exact same
qso twice, it will ignore it the second time. It also detects if you
load the same tq8 file twice. Dupes are created if you change one
field in your entry, though, and there's no way to get rid of
mistakes.


The adif exporter of Fldigi has the option to select which QSO to
export, but there's no automatic "select from this date" (Dave:
wishlist for the future?).

I still rely on xlog for my day to day activity. The ability to manage
multiple log files and still keep them in plain text is unbeatable, to
me. Each month I open a new log file, and load it to lotw at the end
of the month. Files older than a few months (thus unlikely to change
anymore) can be merged into a single annual file.
