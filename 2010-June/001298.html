I strongly suggest that you do not use the same fldigi_def.xml file for 
both the alpha and production executables.  Every attempt is made to 
insure compatibility between the two but no guarantees can be made.
> When I suspected a bad fldigi_def.xml, I used a known good backup. It
> works fine with the alpha but for some reason 3.20.17 has a mind of its
> own and may not read the file or write to it.
>
> Something strange is going on and that may have caused the rig control
> to go south
>
> When you start 3.20.17, does it come up in THOR 5 or your last saved mode?
>   
If you saved a configuration in 3.21.0AF and then started 3.20.17 you 
can expect that the last saved mode will not be correct.  That is 
because new modes (WEFAX) have been added to 3.21.0AF.  That causes a 
translation in the mode tables.
