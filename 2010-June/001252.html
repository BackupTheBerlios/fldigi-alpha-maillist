Would it be possible to have the squelch open when an
RSID signal is received for certain modes, then close
when the data stops?

In our MARS nets many members can't hear the commands
to change modes, and if the squelch would open, it would be
better to copy in poor conditions or with weak signals.

We typically use MT-63 or OLIVIA for sending traffic on
MARS nets. It could be made mode specific or only
for modes that would benefit from an open squench.
Preferably it would be a selectable setting.
