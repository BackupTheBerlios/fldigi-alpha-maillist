Dick Kriss wrote:
> I have been helping another Mac user to get started with fldigi and
> his Icom Pro III audio input to fldigi is too weak to show signals on the
> waterfall.  He has been using another program and the audio level from
> his SignalLink SL-1+ (not the USB version) works fine.  He ask me if
> fldigi with any audio adjustment. I noted the following

If his SL-1+ is rev B or C there is an internal jumper position
that can be used to increase the audio sensitivity.  The information
is on the Tigertronics web site.

I don't personally have an SL-1 but I do have several SignaLink USB
and they also have that provision.
