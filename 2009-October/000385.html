Ok Dick, good to hear you have it all worked out. Guess it might have
been just an OS-X thing.

Don't be shy of the Save button. Many times I've clicked the button when
I haven't made any changes.

I hope everyone keeps in mind this is not an unusual occurence with an
alpha version, and not only with fldigi.

I worked with an alpha for a list member about a year ago. It wasn't too
bad as it is a rather no-frills app. However, I "volunteered" to alpha
test a couple of other apps, different story all together.

Just remember to keep your data backed up, or start a new alpha fldigi
with the --config-dir switch to a non-critical directory.
