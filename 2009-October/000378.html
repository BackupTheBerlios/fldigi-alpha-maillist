Dick,

I'm running AY on Windows XP Pro SP3, and find that of all the various 
modes and sub-modes, RTTY is the only one which reverts to something 
different than it was when I closed Fldigi (the default 170 Hz, 45.5 
Baud).  If I set it to some different sub-mode, close the program, then 
re-open it, the mode comes back to 170/45.5.  It appears that your 
"default" is different than my "default" - strange!

All of this with my radio not transmitting.  So, I tried it with the 
radio turned off, and get the same result.

So, I tried a custom RTTY config, saved the configuration, and after 
closing and re-opening the program, my custom settings came back up!  
After further experimentation, I found that whatever settings are set 
for the RTTY sub-mode when you save your Fldigi configuration, that is 
what will default whenever you open Fldigi in RTTY mode.  Bug?  
Feature?  You decide!  ;-)

73,
-Joe-
