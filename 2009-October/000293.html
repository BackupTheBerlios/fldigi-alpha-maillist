On Wed, Oct 21, 2009 at 8:40 AM, hteller <hteller at comcast.net> wrote:
> Andy, the reason we use DominoEx for NBEMS instead of PSK250, 125, or 63, is
> that noise spikes will pull the frequency in the absence of a signal because
> they look like a signal to the AFC. DominoEx uses no AFC and can be mistuned
> half the mode bandwidth and still copy. PSK250 works OK on VHF FM where
> there are very few noise spikes, but on HF, we have found it does not work
> well except perhaps in the winter when the noise levels, and especially
> static levels, are low.
>
> Hold down CTRL while you roll the mouse wheel and are pointing to the
> waterfall, and you can reduce the capture range of the AFC on fldigi. That
> may help. The
> bandwidth cursor will change size as you roll the wheel.
>
