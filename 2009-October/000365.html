2.            Did we at one point in time have a macro command to turn on
and off or toggle the TXID? If so, I can not locate it now. 

 

If we did not have it then it might be useful to have, so that on a CQ call
the RSID could be toggled on. Then the first exchange macro could turn it
off, so it is not enabled for every transmission when in a QSO. Then the
last SK macro could then toggle the TXID back on so that others can see the
mode in use. 

 

I am thinking  more use of RSID on modes other than PSK31 is necessary, I
still can not differentiate many of the different digital modes in use, and
anything that helps but does not need us to send unnecessary data, and use
the extra bandwidth of the RSID signals all of the time, has its place and
value.
