I notice that not everyone keeps their proper time and date set on their 
operating systems.  I see this quite often when receiving email from 
folks with the wrong dates and times.

I would imagine we would run into folks in the field running FLICS that 
might make this same mistake and if they use the click to add feature 
for time and date, they would be sending erroneous data.

I wonder what the team thinks about having a box pop up showing the 
user's current year, date, time and UTC offset setting with a box that 
asks if they are sure this is correct before the FLICS app can be opened.
