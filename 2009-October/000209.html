I have just finished testing for this problem,  here is my setup.

Mac Mini with Snow Leopard 10.6.1
Fldigi 3.13AN
FT817  5 watts
Miracle Antenna  (used this to set close to the Mac Mini so as to induce

Rascal Interface

MFJ Autotuner.
