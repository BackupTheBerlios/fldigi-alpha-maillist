Lynn,

Yep, the list helped me.  In fldigi configuration, under Modems/PSK you will 
see three tabs: "General", "Mail", and "Viewer".  When transmitting 
keyboard-to-keyboard in fldigi, the AFC settings in "General" are used. 
When transmitting using one of the ARQ programs like flarq or jpskmail, the 
search and AFC setting in "Mail" are used.  Once I adjusted the "Mail" 
values, things started working the way I expected.  The values I set there 
were as recommended: 20 Hz search range, and 20 Hz AFC range, plus checking 
the "Reset to carrier" box.  It made sense once it was explained to me but I 
think it could be made a little more explicit in future updates to those 
particular dialog boxes.
