Dick, I usually find that the 2 controls for signal processing need to
be adjusted. These are the 2 controls on the bottom left of the gui.
Nine times out of 10 this is the problem.

I would have him set whatever OS-X uses for audio control to max and
make sure nothing is muted. Also slide the balance controls to the left
or right to see if that has any effect.
