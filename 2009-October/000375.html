I have been using fldig-3.13AY.dmg and having fun working
new counties.  It has been working great but I just noticed
something I have not seen before.

It was my understanding fldigi would remember the last
used Operator Mode settings after a close and restart.

The current 3.13AY remembers the mode but seems to
have a mind of its own on the  sub mode settings.   For
example, I just finished a RTTY QSO using the first option
on pull down selector of RTTY-45 with Shift 170 and a
Baud 45.5 rate.  When I quit and restarted fldigi, it came
up in RTTY with a Shift of 850 and a Baud of 75.0.  I now
have to be careful to check the sub mode settings.

Anyone else noticed this issue?
