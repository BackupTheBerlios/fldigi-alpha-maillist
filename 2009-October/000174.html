I like the feature under file, where you can load different macro files. 
  I wonder if the same could be done with the fldigi_def file.  Here's 
the reason why I ask.  On our demo's, I use an ACER Netbook and 
sometimes have it hooked up with my Signalink USB but usually have it 
set up to use without an interface.

It's not much of an inconvenience to go into the sound card setting to 
make the change because the calibration figures are about the same 
between my internal sound card and the USB signalink.  But this is not 
the case with w3yj's computer.  To switch between a USB sound card 
device and his internal sound card, he has to make the sound card 
setting change as well as changing the calibration figures.  It would be 
cool to have a few different fldigi_def files to choose from in this 
instance, especially for operators who may disconnect their laptop from 
the home station and their USB Signalink in order to go mobile and use 
the internal sound card.

Just a thought.
