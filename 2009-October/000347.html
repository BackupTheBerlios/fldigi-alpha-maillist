Just a quick reminder that the list address is

  fldigi-alpha at lists.berlios.de

I am reminding you because I have noticed that some messages have been
sent to fldigi-alpha-bounces at lists.berlios.de instead.  The bounce
address is meant to collect delivery failure reports ("bounces") from
mail servers and not receive normal list traffic.  This mechanism allows
the mailing list software to notice if a member address has become
invalid so that it can stop sending.

I really don't mind receiving the bounce messages, which get forwarded
to the admin addresses, but you should know that any mail sent to the
-bounce address gets archived in /dev/null :-)
