Hallo David,

I found a problem with the QSY button.

My rig is a FT-100 with rigcat. The tests were made on 20m, QRG 14070
with PSK 31.

When I QSY to a frequency higher than the defined  sweet spot, and
thereafter undo this QSY with a right mouse button click, the frequency
on my rig and in the PSK Browser is set correct. The frequency shown on
the left side of the waterfall is about 14071 instead of 14070 as
before. When I call a stored frequency like 21070 the rig and the PSK
Browser are set correctly, but the offset of the waterfall is the same.
A QSY to a frequency  lower than the sweet spot does not show this
problem, it is even solving the wrong indication from a previous QSY to
a higher frequency.
