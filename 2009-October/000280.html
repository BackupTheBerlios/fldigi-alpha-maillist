This is a heads up to Alpha testers.  I installed fldigi 3.13AS
(.dmg version) this evening to check out the new UI.

The new interface looked good but as part of my first test I
responded to a RTTY CQ and had a good QSO.  At the end of
the QSO I used my normal log macro to save the QSO to the
logbook. Fldigi-3.13AS crashed big time while trying to write
the QSO to the logbook.

The QSO was not saved; however, I managed to get a copy
of crash report to Dave (before the dumb Snow Leopard sent
it to Apple) to see if it offered a clue.

Try to save a test QSO to the logbook before you get to involved
with really using 3.13AS. After it crashed a 2nd time, I removed
it from my hard drive and reverted to 3.13AR.
