Dave,  

> Chip is actually not too bad of a mode and is pretty good for keyboard comms on 
> 80m.

For what it's worth... 

HF path simulations indicate that Chip-64 might not be the best mode for 80 meters when selective fading / static crashes become an issue. 

It might not be an apples-to-apples comparison, but Contestia 16/500 would be a better keyboard mode alternative since it has the same word-per-minute rate and much better throughput under adverse conditions. 

Have a look at the HF path simulations below. You'll notice that Chip 64 is still taking hits with a +6db signal-to-noise ratio and the print is about the same as RTTY under the same conditions. 

Just a thought. 

Tony -K2MO
