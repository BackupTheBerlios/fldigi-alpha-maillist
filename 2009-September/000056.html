Greetings,

Gary (N0GW) and I just tested on 80m fldigi-3.13AE on Windows, and a 
problem we were having with PSK250 has been solved in this version.

Testing last weekend we found that with PSK250 and FLArq, the <EOT> was 
being truncated.  The PTT was dropping too soon and the last bit of 
audio was not sending.  Dave added a delay at the end, and now it has a 
little bit of a tail, so all the audio is transmitting as it should.  
File transfers, etc. all worked perfectly, and it is noticeably faster 
not having to run RSID to keep the PTT up (which is what we had to do 
before).

I'll do some more testing otherwise, but with the quick test all seems good.

Thanks for the fix Dave!
