Hello Dave and the devl team.

Skipped AG and went straight to AH. A cursory bit of testing shows that 
the QRZ lookup problem is fixed (yeah!). Also I am having no problems 
with either RigCAT or Hamlib and my FT-2000 with the following settings:

Hamlib:
   Retries: 0
   Write Delay: 5
   Post Write Delay: 1


RigCAT:
   Retries: 2
   Retry Interval: 500 ms
   Write Delay: 5

If I set the retries to 0 in RigCat I get a bunch of "W: rigCAT_getmode: 
Retries failed" to stdout. Once it locked up fldigi.

Many thanks Dave and team.
