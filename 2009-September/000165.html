Paul,

I assume you mean when using the PSK modes. If so, in the modem/psk
configuration tab, adjust the signal acquisition S/N up (I found 13dB was ok
for me) and the search range down ( had to put it at 10Hz). The upcoming
version of Fldigi (3.13, when it comes out) seems to have resolved that
jitter issue. In the mean time this is the workaround.

Regards, John
