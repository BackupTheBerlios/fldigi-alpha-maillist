I wouldn't use Pulse with fldigi unless I had no other choice. Port
Audio plays best with fldigi.

From a terminal ::

sudo apt-get build-dep fldigi

should bring in the necessary Pulse and Port audio libs needed.

You will need the Pulse Audio Volume Manager gui.
