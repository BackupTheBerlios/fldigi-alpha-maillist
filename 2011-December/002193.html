I think it depends on the local emergency communication "rules." Here in 
Virginia the hams at the state EOC only want 80/40 meter phone, 
Winlink2k (yet they only use telnet to pick up traffic so with the 
internet down that is gone), and packet. So here we have to use packet 
via a couple nodes to drop off written messages in the state EOC BBS. 
Having a soundcard packet capability as part of the fldigi suite means 
one less piece of software and hardware for our go kits. For those of us 
with radios that include 2m it makes a good package. That said in our 
local county EOC and alternate EOC we still have hardware TNCs.

HF packet or VHF packet is still useful for us.

73, tom n4zpt
