I don't understand the recent interest in packet. It was a big deal in the late 80's and I even hold HF Packet WAS Certificate #5. I gave away my PK-232's. 

What's with all of the renewed interest?  I have no plan to ever use ax.25 again and do not appreciate the need for adding it into fldigi.
