Here's an idea for the development team based on some recent 
experiences. For use on repeaters, we have to be very careful of longer 
messages/data with regard to repeater timeouts, which are usually set to 
2 or 3 minutes. Also, some of our ops on HF, including myself, have 
underestimated the amount of time it takes to send larger messages or 
spreadsheets on a given mode.

So, would it be possible to have FLDIGI provide a calculations (via 
pop-up or separate display button) in min/sec once a file is dropped 
into the tx window?
