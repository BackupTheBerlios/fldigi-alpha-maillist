> But I still do not understand why the new tag, when as far as I can 
> tell it works exactly the same as the <LOG> does.
no it is different.

<LOG> executes the save to logbook when the function key is pressed.
<LNW> executes the save to logbook when the "^L" is encountered in 
transmit stream.

The two can differ by a minute (or perhaps more) when you are 
transmitting in a very slow baud rate modem, and you have a lot of 
transmit buffer text preceding the "^L".
