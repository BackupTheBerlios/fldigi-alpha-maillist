As Dave points out this problem has been observed by Mac OS X users.  Please
speak up if you 
have observed the issue on other platforms.

The issue (fldigi quits or crashes) only when the Reed-Solomon ID (Tx) is
active with a check
in box to Send at EOT as well as mode in the Transmit modes selector.  The
TxID works great 
then for no good reason fldigi quits or crashes.  The workaround is uncheck
the Send at EOT
box.
