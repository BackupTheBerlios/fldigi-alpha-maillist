Dave,

I don't think ARQ would really be applicable for the time calculation 
since you would have no idea how many retries you might run into. I 
guess you could do an ARQ calc based on perfect copy.

With a WRAP, it's just a single transmission and the time calculation 
should always prove to be dead on based on the length of message and 
mode/submode.
