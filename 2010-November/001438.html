Hello all,

Here's a few requests/suggestions for the rx window of FLDIGI:

1) Ability to do shortcut key ctrl+f, or right click to bring up an 
option to do a keyword search of the raw received text.

2) When right clicking in the RX window, and after clicking "clear", 
have an additional window pop up to ask "are you sure you want to clear 
all text". The "copy" option is just above the "clear" option, and in a 
rush on a few occasions, I managed to accidentally clear all my rx text 
when I was shooting for "copy". I know we have the option for the 
capture rx text file, but I think the extra window popping up to ask the 
user to confirm clear would be good.

On a somewhat different subject, a while ago I proposed that when you 
click on Op Mode then arrow down and click on the main mode selection, 
this would select the last used submode, rather than having to arrow 
over to click the specific submode.

One last request/suggestion... can 4/500 Olivia be added to the submode 
selections of Olivia?

-Dave, KB3FXI
