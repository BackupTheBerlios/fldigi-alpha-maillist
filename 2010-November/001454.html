Hack AA5VU has addressed the time on / time off issue more clearly than I
did, so I won't go into it here, but I want to clarify about the macros.

Suppose you click or type into the TX window, even a few spaces, which are
totally invisible in the TX window. (Or you run a macro that outputs
spaces or something else.) Then you search & pounce but when you pounce
your CW does nothing, or your RTTY sends a bunch of spaces, or worse yet,
it sends the text you've accidentally pasted into the TX buffer.   So I
will make my contest macros do this:
  <CLEARTX><TX>...
before they launch into the exchange, return, etc.
