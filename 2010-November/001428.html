I understand. At the risk of being callous, I suspect that this problem will
be self-correcting with time. People die and their old ways die with them.
CW is interesting and fun but in no way does it make sense today for message
handling.

Ham radio almost doesn't have a reason to exist anymore. Its last vestige of
use to the world which would dearly love to reuse the allocated spectrum for
something else, is EmComm. If amateur radio doesn't conform to the best
engineering practices of the day, that will go away too.

So, are any of the folks here going around the NTS on their own to work with
their local, regional, state, or federal agencies directly? I know that is
impolitic to suggest but I suspect there is the occasional disgruntled op
who would like to leapfrog the enmired, entrenched, and embedded
establishment. If talking about this openly bothers you, please contact me
directly off-list.
