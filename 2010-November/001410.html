As far as the 205 form is concerned there are two versions in use
predominantly both of which are accepted versions. What we have to decide is
which template we feel best meets the needs. The formats are the same but
wording as you noted varies.  These also may change as we move forward and
Federal Agencies continue to evolve in response requirements.

We probably need more feedback from our EmComm and Agency users to guide us
as to most updated changes.

This is a good example. I have 3 different ICS form tools provided by
different groups FEMA being one and all have some variances from form to
form. The formats are all the same but wordings may vary.

I look forward to everyones input.

Thanks for bringing this to the forefront.
