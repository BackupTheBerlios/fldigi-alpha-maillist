ARQ is fine and well on a one to one connection. We use MT63 1k long on 
HF and MT63 2k long on VHF/UHF FM with great success using wrap. The big 
advantage is that an unlimited number of receiving stations can all 
receive from a single transmission and independently extract and verify 
the messages.
