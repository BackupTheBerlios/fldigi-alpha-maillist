Stephane F8CFE

No tight integration but exporting to LoTW is very easy. 

Just go to Logbook/ADIF/Export, select the QSO's you want to
upload to LoTW on the left, the options you want on right and 
click OKAY. This will let you save an ADIF formatted file that
that is ready to be signed using the ARRL's tqsl.app.
