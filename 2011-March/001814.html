Good news and bad news with the new Kenwood TS-590S firmware version 1.0.3
See this link for the changes

http://www.kenwood.com/i/products/info/amateur/ts_590/ts590_update_e.html

Bad News:  RigCAT no longer works.  It was my default for Rig Control 
before the update and after the update I cannot get it to initialize.

Good News:  Hamlib not only works but fldigi-3.21.7 now displays both VFO's. I 
was trying to work a DX station on RTTY and it was nice to see fldigi display the 
frequency for both VFO's.

There is nothing I could see in the firmware v1.0.3 that can explain why RigCAT
no longer works and why fldigi started supporting both VFO's with Hamlib
