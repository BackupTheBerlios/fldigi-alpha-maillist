I downloaded flrig-1.0.1.1Q.dmg this morning for test drive
with the Kenwood TS-590S using Mac OS X.  The application
installed okay; however,  it had issues with the TS-590S. I had 
fldigi-3.21.4 running at the same time using XML-RPC for Rig 
Control.

I could adjust the Volume, the Mic gain and Power.  The Att
and Pre buttons worked and the NB worked with NB1 but not
with the other NB 2 on the TS-590.  Nada on frequency, S-Meter, 
VFO or any of the items above the volume line.

I am not a big fan of computer rig control and can live with
the standard fldigi Hamlib Rig Control but would like to get 
flrig working just to prove that it works because new TS-590's 
are being delivered each week and we will be seeing more 
fldigi/TS-590S users.

I will try again with the next flrig release.
