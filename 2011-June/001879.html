On Jun 1, 2011, at 9:20 PM, Dick Kriss wrote:

> The following are my two wish list items for fldigi:
> 
> 
> - Add an fldigi audio output slider
> 
> Fldigi's raw audio output is pretty strong and has to be reduced to 
> control the ALC.  The dumb Menu #64 audio input control on my 
> TS-590S has 10 step functions rather than a POT. Step #2 is too low 
> (no ALC is displayed on the S-Meter) and Step #3 is too high (full 
> scale ALC on the S-Meter). The only way I have to attenuate the 
> fldigi audio output is to use the OS X System Preferences Sound/Output 
> panel and the volume output slider is already near the left side giving 
> me little control. Having an audio output slider in fldigi would be a 
> welcome addition.
> 

My wish has been granted with the new TX Attenuator added by Dave on 4-June-2011

I could not figure out the shortcut on the Mac; however, the slider under Fldigi configuration/Audio/TxLevel works perfect.  

I cranked in -10.0 Tx Atten (db) and now have the OS X System Preferences/Sound/Output panel Output volume at 41%.  Before having the fldig TX Attenuator, I had to run the OS X Output volume to the far left that was close to being OFF.

I am now showing zero ALC on the TS-590's S-Meter and at close to full power on the
external power meter at 50.300 MHz.  I don't think I will QRM anyone testing on six meters.

I still need to do some more testing on other bands. Base on this smoke test I think the new TX attenuator is going to be a nice addition to fldigi.

Thanks Dave!

73 Dick AA5VU
