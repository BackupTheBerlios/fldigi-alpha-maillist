upper-left-hand corner of the fldigi frame is not of much use when
displaying VFO frequency. It is duplicate information. Having it display the
center of the emitted signal is far more useful. The information at the top

resolution. The QSO Freq field has the information one needs but that would
be better displayed in the main frequency display.
