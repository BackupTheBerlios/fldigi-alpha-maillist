I understand that new users might be confused, especially if they do not 
take the time to read the on-line help.  My wife thinks I am a perfectly 
anal engineer because I always read the manual for a new car ... from 
front to back and then from back to front.  She never opens one up.  I 
know how to use every control on the car.  She constantly asks me how to 
work the windshield wipers and the lights.

default value.  It is detailed in the owner's manual.  Just read it and 
insist that your new users do so also.  If they want to fly the airplane
