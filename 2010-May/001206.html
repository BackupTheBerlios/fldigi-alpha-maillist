I have had the same error, on a low computing power EEEpc. I determined that
the problem was that there was too much graphics utilization of the cpu ,
for drawing the waterfall. I had htop running so I could watch which
processes were taking up the cycles. There was always high graphics
interactivity when the audio chain timed out. You could try reducing the
vertical and horizontal sizes of the waterfall to see if that helps.

This is consistent with a receive only situation when the problem occurs, as
during transmit there is so much less graphics use even if you have monitot
transmit turned on.
