Disagreed with all...

My point is only on the initial startup and why in the world you would 
display a frequency that is neither entered by the user or pulled from 
their rig???

3.583 display upon the load of the software implies that there is some 
sort of rig control being deployed, and it's confusing to new users. It 
even confuses people who are not complete idiots.

Upon start up on a new install, what in the world would be the problem 
with having what appears to be a rig control display show all zeros when 
  there is no rig control being deployed???????????????????????

 From there, if a user changes the frequency, by all means... have it 
save that figure... but why have a random frequency appear in the 
display when there is no rig control deployed.
