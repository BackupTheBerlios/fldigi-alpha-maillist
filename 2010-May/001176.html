Two points here:

1. If you have two displays, they should display the same thing if
they are supposed to display the same thing. Anything else is
confusing to the operator.

2. The frequency display on the radios is almost never the actual
frequency of the transmission. Fldigi does calculate and display the
actual center frequency only in the log window.

Request: I would like the option of having the main fldigi display
show the actual transmission center frequency for the mode.
