Hi Everyone,

The Western North Carolina State Medical Assistance Team 6 will be involved
in a mock disaster drill beginning at
17:00 (5:00 PM) EDT today 7 May 2010.

As part of this exercise I will be acting as a forward deployed unit working
from Cape Hatteras and establishing
communication back to the Main Team in Asheville NC across the state.

I would like to incorporate digital communications in my testing this
evening from 19:00 - 22:00 (7 to 10 PM) EDT.

I propose to work either 20 M at 14071.00 and/or 30 meters at 10140.00 using
PSK 250R or Domino EX11.
This will provide base data for deployment plans and response for our
network.

If any members of the Alpha Testing team would be interested in
participation please let me know.

I propose to send traffic out of our area to another station and provide
information regarding forwarding via Internet.
Use of Flics and Wrap would be very nice and one reason for picking the two
modes we have.  Throughput for one
and reliability of the other.

If anyone would like to join in the fun you would be most welcome.

Thanks for all your work making Fldigi the success that it is.
