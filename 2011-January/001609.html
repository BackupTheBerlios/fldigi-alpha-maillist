I upgraded this morning to fldigi 3.21.0BA and within fldigi v.0.8.6 
from jpskmail.jar dated 2011.01.02 to jpskmail.jar dated 2011.01.09 and 
several amazing events happened under Ubuntu 10.04-1LTS :
- my SignaLink USB was not anymore properly recognized (Audio Codec 
choice possible in Configure - Sound Board only in "Playback", it did 
not show in "Capture" and a "Connect" or "Pos." in jPSKMail did not 
trigger transmission in fldigi ;
- I reinstalled 3.21.0AZ and got my audiocodec in both capture and 
playback but no transmission triggered ;
- I reinstalled fldigi 0.20.24 with no success ;
- I reinstalled several times these various versions without any success ;
- I reinstalled the former jpskmail.jar dated 01.02 without improvement ;
- I then rebooted to Windows XP SP3 where I upgraded fldigi to 3.21.0BA 
and jPSKMail v.0.8.6 to jpskmail.jar dated 01.09. Both programs worked 
nicely on Windows.

I rebooted the computer to Ubuntu and tried my last setting : fldigi 
3.21.0AZ and jPSKMail v.0.8.6 with jpskmail.jar dated 01.02 and it 
worked nicely now, audiocodec being recognized. I upgraded again to 
fldigi 3.21.0BA and jPSKMail v.0.8.6 with jpskmail.jar dated 01.09 and 
both programs also worked nicely now.

Well, I don't understand what happened actually but after reinstallation 
and reboot of the computer fldigi 3.21.0BA can now recognize my 
SignaLink USB both under Windows and Ubuntu and can work with jPSKMail 
v.0.8.6 with jpskmail.jar dated 01.09.
