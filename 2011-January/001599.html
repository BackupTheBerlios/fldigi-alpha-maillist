Joe,

Yes, as Mike mentioned, the browser searches for a match to your 
specified string and if it is found it highlights the line. The string 
selection is made in the config settings under Config->User 
Interface->Browser->Seek Regular Expression.

And it appears that the explanation of it is missing from the current 
version of the manual (i.e. the PSK Viewer). There are a couple of 
hyperlinks to "psk viewer" but their target is missing. That explains 
why you couldn't find it.
