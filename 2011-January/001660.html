I think the only digital sound card software I've ever used that allows for
the shift on MT63 is MixW, which was my primary software before NBEMS/FLDIGI
came along. I didn't like the idea of the fixed bottom frequency of 500 hz
when the gang added the MT63 modem, but I've come to appreciate it now.
Like Harry said, it eliminates the possibility of someone not being on the
right spot on the waterfall, especially when we're on FM.
