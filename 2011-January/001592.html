I've been thinking about this, and have possibly a better idea...

How about making the click on a line of decoded text a macro function.  
For instance, enable an "on-click, execute the following macro commands" 
- Thus it becomes user tailorable (especially if the RIGMODE and RIGFLTR 
commands are implemented).  So, I could click on a line of decoded text 
and build a macro to have it go to the sweet spot and turn on a 
particular rig mode and filter width.  Sweet in more ways than one!
