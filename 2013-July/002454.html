Very interesting. I found a workaround to fldigi-3.21.72BM hang on Exit issue. 

The OS X Apple menu "Quit Fldigi" works and fldigi does not hang.  Also, the keyboard shortcut "Cmd-Q" works and fldigi does not hang.



The fldigi application menu "File/Exit" item hangs on exit as well as the red-dot. The fldigi Exit "X" shortcut does not seem to be active under OS X.



This may be clue on something to check for the next alpha release.
