This update is for the TenTec Jupiter/Pegasus transceivers

Version 0.9.99N
   
    TT550 Vfo Adjust
   
        * Added ability to correct for master oscillator offset.
          Correction entered in ppm using counter control
        * Tested computation using 5, 10 and 15 MHz WWV transmission.

My Jupiter measured +1.2 ppm for the master oscillator correction, or 12 
Hz at 10 MHz.  fldigi's Analysis modem was used to monitor WWV while 
adjusting the oscillator offset.
