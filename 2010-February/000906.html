Roger,

Do you have psk31 and psk63 enabled for detection?  Those work 
flawlessly on my bench tests.

Please keep in mind that the RSID signal might act as noise if the RSID 
is disabled.  RSID on psk31 or even psk63 is rather like tying a pink 
ribbon on a pig's snout.  I wish that operators would cease and desist 
from the practice.
