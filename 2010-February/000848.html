Got back into digital after a long while being off.

I successfully married up fldigi with PowerSDR running a Softrock TXRX 6.3 using virtual audio cables and virtual com ports for rig control.  Worked a little bit of the WPX RTTY test, made about 25 Q's on 40 meters while runningabt 15 watts (using an HFPack amp) into a hustler mobile whip on a metal building roof.

Running 3.13BX.
