Are you using the "fake" (no rig) rigcat?  I suspect than in both cases
you are in the wrong sideband.  The RSID decoder expects that fldigi's
idea of the current sideband is also the actual rig sideband, which is
normally true.  However, in XMLRPC/norig modes, you can be decoding
audio received in USB mode by the rig, but with fldigi's rig mode set to
LSB (e.g. via the menu under the frequency display).  In that case the
RSID decoder will do the wrong thing and not decode anything.
