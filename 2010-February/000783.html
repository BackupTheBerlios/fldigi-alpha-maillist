Another approach would be to turn on the "Always Show Audio Frequencies" 
feature found in Waterfall->Display configuration. That is what we do here. 
Of course, then you must specify the both the radio frequency and the audio 
frequency to tell people what frequency to go to.  The problem we ran into 
is that most stations run software that does not display frequency as the 
radio's frequency plus or minus the audio frequency, depending upon mode. 
The frequency we are usually given is the transceiver dial frequency and if 
we are lucky, sometimes the audio frequency. Having the waterfall show the 
audio frequency only has worked better for us.
