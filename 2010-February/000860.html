I just installed flrig-0.9.99O.dmg as a test and had it working
with fldigi-3.13BY.dmg and my Kenwood TS-570S using the
XML-RPC Rig Control option.

The two applications seemed to work together until I ran
into some issues:

- The NB button did not talk to the rig.  The other buttons
seemed to work okay.

-  I was copying a strong RTTY station on the waterfall
and clicking on the fldigi QSY button works but does
not move the VFO on the rig.  The QSY function seems
to be disabled when operating under XML-RPC rig control.

Flrig finally crashed with a "hang" error report
