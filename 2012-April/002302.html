1.  multiple ^r in the Tx buffer behavior is as follows:

2.  When transmit starts the outgoing text will stop at the first ^r 
(the sent text should clear from the Tx buffer)

3.  You can then wait for a reply, scratch your nose, or get a cup of 
coffee.

4.  Press T/R (or Pause/Break) and transmit will start again and 
continue until the tx pointer encounters the next ^r.

2 .. 4 repeats for as many times as ^r appears in the Tx buffer.

Let me know if it does not behave in this manner.
