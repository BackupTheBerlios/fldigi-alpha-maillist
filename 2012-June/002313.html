I had a QSO with CA2LQA and logged it as 1718 using fldigi-3-21.43, then opened it with fldigi-3.21.43AC and as expected the time was displayed as 171800.  I manually changed Time to 171812 and clicked on the Update button to save the time. I then exported the .adif file and sent the QSO to LoTW as 17:18:12Z

<QSO_DATE:10>2012-06-01
<QSO_TIME:9>17:18:12Z

That is the good news.  The bad news is I quit flidig-3.21.43AC and two hours later opened it again and 
the time has reverted to old 171800.  

Has anyone else noticed anything like this where the Update button failed to write the time?

I wish the fldigi Logbook display of the Time included separators. Time was easy to read when it was displayed in the normal 4-digit UTC format.  The six numbers with no separators is hard to read.
