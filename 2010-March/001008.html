I have a backup laptop (5yo panasonic toughbook) that only gets turned 
on about 4 times a year and  has no wireless, so it wouldn't pick up the 
update from the internet time servers automatically.  It would be VERY 
easy for me to fire this up in a flooding situation on Monday (if we 
were called out), after the time change, and not notice the time was off.

Also, several of the County EOC's I've dealt with, do not want the 
RACES/ARES computers on their network, so the auto internet time updates 
would be out there, too.

-Dave, KB3FXI
