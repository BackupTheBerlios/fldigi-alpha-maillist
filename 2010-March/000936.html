On Sat, Mar 6, 2010 at 1:16 PM, Dick Kriss <aa5vu at arrl.net> wrote:
> Since fldigi has been released to beta is there a need for an
> alpha list?

Yes, for the next alpha as development continues. I don't know any
programmers who are satisfied with leaving well enough alone. There is
always an idea for a new feature or something done better. ;-)

With the growth of SDR the soundcard is going to go away as the
"radio" will provide a bitstream of IF samples. Right now these are
handled through some sort of interprocess communications that require
the radio and CODEC to run as tasks under the same OS. In the future
those bitstreams will travel across the network allowing functions to
be distributed across multiple machines.

So, yes, there will continue to be a need for an alpha list to provide
feedback on the new features. ;-)
