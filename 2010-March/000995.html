> This is a pretty easy problem to solve without involving fldigi.  If
> your machince is connected to the internet, you can just set up an NTP
> client and forget about the issue.  It will keep your clock to within a
> few milliseconds of the NTP server, accounting for drift and avoiding
> large increments.
>

>
>  http://www.boulder.nist.gov/timefreq/service/pdf/win2000xp.pdf
>

> Then you should make good timekeeping part of your SOP.  NTP is the
> simplest solution.  It's much better for fldigi to assume that the time

> independent manner.
