Greetings,

I had the PSK browser open, and it work great when in BPSK31 mode.  
However, a station called in BPSK125 and I left the browser open but 
changed from 31 to 125 to copy and have a QSO with him.  He had a 599 
signal to my station, but I was only getting partial decodes.  After 
several minutes of trying to figure out what was wrong, I closed the PSK 
browser window and suddenly got perfect decode, as I would expect with 
such a strong signal.  While decoding him I re-opened the PSK browser 
window, and the decode immediately scrambled, but unscrambled when I 
again closed the browser window.

Not too big a deal since the PSK browser window in mostly for BPSK31 
anyway, but should probably be fixed.

I'm running FLdigi 3.20.0b7 on Windows XP Pro SP3.
