David,

This might be a good option for some - in cases where there is not a 
live network connection where NTP can be used to automatically update 
the time. For those of us who usually have a network connection, it can 
be disabled.

For instance, during Field Day, we don't have a network connection at 
our remote location, and it would be good to verify accurate time.
