>>>>> On Sat, 6 Mar 2010 01:09:45 -0700, Anony Mous <fyngyrz at gmail.com> said:

> Under OS X, I often run SSTV, FAX and RTTY software (fldigi) all at once. I
> can pause the SSTV and FAX software to free up my CPU for the other two
> programs; is there a pause feature in fldigi?

You could send fldigi SIGSTOP and SIGCONT. Try these in a terminal with
fldigi running:

  killall -STOP fldigi

or

  killall -CONT fldigi

Of course you won't be able to use it at all while it is suspended.
When it resumes, fldigi will complain that the audio stream timed out
but it should be able to recover.

> If not, then please consider this a feature request. Turn off the waterfall,
> just wait until a mode is picked before consuming CPU constantly.

The waterfall can be paused by clicking on the speed button next to the
frequency readout under the waterfall.  The speeds are: NORM, FAST,
PAUSE, SLOW.
