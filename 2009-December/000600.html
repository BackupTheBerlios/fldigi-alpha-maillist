Well, the way it is supposed to work:

1) flrig reads the current mode and bandwidth from the transceiver
2) flrig sets the saved last mode and bandwidth
3) on exit flrig restores the settings read in 1)

It works that way on my TenTec ArgoV, the Yaesu FT-450 and the Icom 
IC-746Pro.  That tells me that you are seeing something specific to the 
FT857D / 897D transceivers.  I'll look at the support code.
