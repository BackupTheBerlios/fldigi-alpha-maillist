> I noticed today that the  RxID turns off when you acknowledge the

> Also is it possible to sen the RSID on demand in a macro? I see where
> you can turn RSID on, off or toggle, but not just send it regardless
> of it's current state. In other programs I have macros that send RSID
> on CQ's or initial call up but once the QSO is established I no longer
> send it.
>
> <TX>
> <TXID>

Suggest you might use the existing 3.13BG macro tags as follows:

Call CQ with RSID

<TXRSID:on><TX>CQ CQ CQ de <MYCALL> <MYCALL> <MYCALL><RX>

QSO exchange

<TXRSID:off><TX><CALL> <CALL> de <MYCALL>

Turning TXRSID off if it is already off will be ignored.  You can safely 
that tag to the beginning any macro which you want to associate with 
RSID repression.

The various programs that support a macro language do not implement them 
in the same manner.  So what works for one program may not for another 
and vice versa.
