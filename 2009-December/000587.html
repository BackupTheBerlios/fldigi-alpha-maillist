Hi Charles,

No, not implying that at all.
My concern is that the actual text appears as encrypted text
when it is sent and received or intercepted on fldigi.

Winlink does not show up at any stage of its transmission
 or reception as compressed text.

I would be happy to pass FLWRAP up the chain of command to CHNAVMARCORMARS
for a decision on its use. I think I will do that in any case.

To my knowledge FLWRAP has never officially been acknowledged
by the Chief for use on Navy-Marine Corps circuits. If he says it is OK
to use, that's fine with me.

The problem is that not everyone uses fldigi or WRAP or FLWRAP.
Sending traffic on a net with 20 members would cause the TREP to send
all traffic 2 times. Most likely what would happen is that FLWRAP would
simply not be used in its current configuration.

I am all for making traffic go faster, but for MARS (NAVMARCORMARS)
all traffic on normal traffic nets are text files written on ascii text editors.

Please don't get me wrong, I am all for compression but I think
sending compressed text needs to be OK'd by the boss.
