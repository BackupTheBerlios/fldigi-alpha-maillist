I wish there was a quick way to change shift in RTTY, or maybe it could
adapt automatically to shift that is near the selected value.  There
seem to be a lot of perverts out there sending RTTY with 200 Hz shift,
which fldigi will copy when set for 170 but not optimally.

What used to be the gold standard for RTTY was K2STI's RITTY program.
As I recall it would automatically adjust to shifts that were reasonably
close to the current one, and also there were hotkeys to increase and
decrease the shift.
