RSID alerts. So many people are now sending RSIDs on 30m with PSK31 that
alerts pop up all the time. For me it would certainly be useful to have the
other psk speeds and other modes alerted only. If there was a toggle for
each mode in the modem configuration pages that would make fldigi the bees
knees.
