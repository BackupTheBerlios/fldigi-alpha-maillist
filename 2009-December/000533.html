Mike,

Are you building fldigi from source or installing it pre-packaged?

There are some reports that Gnome-system-monitor may give
inaccurate numbers, so could you please do the following and tell
me what you see:

in a terminal window while fldigi is running, type "top" and
see what the percentages are for the top few processes (on average -
it will jump around).

Also, if you or anyone ever wants to open a bug for Ubuntu against
fldigi, you can do it in a terminal window by typing:

ubuntu-bug fldigi

This will gather a bunch of information about your system that
may be useful in troubleshooting. I get notification of those
bugs automatically, and then if you want to post a bug link
here on the list everyone will be able to see your system
information, which will reduce the number of questions we have
to ask.

Thanks,
