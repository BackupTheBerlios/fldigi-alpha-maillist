Hallo David,

All alpha versions compile without any error for openSUSE 11.0, 11.1 and
11.2 and are working on my test systems.
I noticed that the desktop files of flics and flrig have the same words
as flwrap for  GenericName.
I suggest to change the entries:

Name=Flics
GenericName=Forms management editor

Name=Flrig
GenericName=Rig Control

The patched desktop files, which I use for my openSUSE repository, are
attached to this mail.

Flrig, which I consider as a very useful application, has one bug with
my FT-100 transceiver.
The indicated bandwidth of the filter is showing 6000 instead of 2400
and vice versa. When 6000 is selected at the transceiver it is showing 2400.
The FT100D.cxx with this bug solved and the ft-100.diff file I used for
my openSUSE uploads are also also attached.

http://download.opensuse.org/repositories/home:/dl8fcl/


73, Walter DL8FCL
