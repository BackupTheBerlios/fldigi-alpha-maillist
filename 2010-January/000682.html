Version 3.13BN
    
    Modem Initialization
    
        Removed duplicate invocations of init() for several
        modem types.
    
    Carrier callback
    
        Corrected frequency limits computation in waterfall::opmode()
        method.

    CSV Export
    
        Error corrected in export MYXCHG field.

The modem initialization and carrier callback bug's were noticed by 
Fred, DL6XAZ,** and confirmed by others.  It was most noticeable when 
using the scroll wheel to flip through the modem types.  It effected 
modem initialization every time a new modem type was selected, but was 
just not so easily discerned.  This was a grand slam report from the 
testers as the offending code has been with us for quite some time.
