Depends on the upper and lower limit settings in the FFT Processing tab 
under Waterfall. My settings are 450 and 2400. I adjusted the right 
border to just barely eliminate the black space. This gives me a 2400Hz 
waterfall width. This also fixes the QSY shift.

Once you set the upper and lower limits, try adjusting the right border 
  width.
