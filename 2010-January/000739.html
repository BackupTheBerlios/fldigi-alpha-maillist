> The designed behavior is to center the waterfall at the last audio 
> sub-carrier point on a restart.  If the entire waterfall can be 
> displayed then no shifting occurs.  If not (most prevalent on x2 or x4 
> WF expansion) then the waterfall start frequency will be shifted .  This 
> is not a QSY, but simply a re-centering of the WF display.  I prefer the 
> current implementation.  I suppose another configuration item could be 
> added to the waterfall configuration tabs to allow selection of the 
> startup behavior.  Restoring the exact WF positioning on restart can 
> also be implemented if the testers say that is less confusing.  Let the 
> group know what your preference is.
