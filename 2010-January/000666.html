>>>>> On Wed, 06 Jan 2010 06:25:55 -0500, Al Danis <w6hgf at cox.net> said:

> This one has been difficult to find because it happened only randomly it
> seemed.  But I finally found the trigger that causes it.  When I take
> the mouse and left click into the blue transmit pane, everything works
> fine.  However if the mouse moves down into the waterfall and brings up
> the yellow freq markers, even though the left button has not been
> pressed to move the frequency, the caps stop, but the lowercase keep
> going.

This happens because the waterfall "steals" the modifier presses (shift,
control, etc.) when it has the mouse pointer.  It uses those modifier
keys mainly for shortcuts that move the frequency cursor.  This way
there is no need to click inside the waterfall (potentially changing
frequency) before being able to use those shortcuts.

So yes, the mouse pointer needs to be inside the transmit text.  I will
see if this can be improved.
