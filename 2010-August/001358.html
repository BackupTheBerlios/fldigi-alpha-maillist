The port, baud rate, and whether you are using RTS or DTR to power your 
interface should be the same in both.

You need to have Commands are echoed checked. Some users check RTS/CTS 
flow control, if you have, uncheck it.

Set retries to 2, retry interval to 50, and write delay to 10.

Whose design did you follow for the CI-V interface ? I've seen just 
about all ot them. They tend to be basically the same.
