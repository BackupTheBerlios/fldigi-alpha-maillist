Ed--

I have all of the suggested settings below, though I have been
playing with different values for retry interval, # of retries,
and delay to determine if this would change anything. So far,
no luck.

With no RTS/CTS or DTR/DSR lines, it did not make sense to
have any of those boxes checked.

The interface is based on one that I found on the web, which
was in turn based on one in the ARRL Handbook. I am using an
RS-232 chip with two Tx and two Rx sections. Wiring is as
follows:

- Radio to TTL Tx1, Tx2, and Rx1
- Computer to RS-232 Tx1 and Rx1
- Other devices to TTL Rx2
- RS-232 Tx2 wired to Rx2

Also, I am using an external wall-wart to a 5v regulator,
so no power is being pulled from the RS-232 interface.

This design isolates the radio from the rest of the CI-V equip-
ment. I was having issues with the TTL signal being pulled to zero
volts when my radio and SteppIR were connected to the same Tx
and Rx inputs when I had the radio set to transceive on the
CI-V line.
