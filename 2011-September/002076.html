then it should be symmetrical. In the case of the K2, it uses different
filters and different injection frequencies. The wide SSB filter is used for
TX but it uses the adjustable CW filter when doing narrow passbands.

But, like I said, I got tired of dealing with that and no longer have a
problem with filter nonlinearity.
