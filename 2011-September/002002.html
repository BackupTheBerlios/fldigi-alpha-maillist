Once again ( but no so often as to be a pest ) I am requesting that AMP ( Amateur Multicast Protocol ) be included someday in FLDIGI.

Here's the protocol:  http://www.spar-hams.org/docs/amp-protocol.html

Here's the existing program that uitilizes AMP: http://www.uspacket.org/Misc/AltCast.zip

Here's some general ( and specific ) information about amateur multicast: http://uspacket.org/network/index.php?PHPSESSID=0ace8584bdfe09af989e90083c1381c3&board=5.0


The current AMP software has three PSK modes, PSK31, QPSK31, and PSK63. These have served well in proving the concept - so now I am hoping to see AMP in FLDIGI, where there are more digital modes to work with.

Respectfully submitted,

73 DE Charles, N5PVL
