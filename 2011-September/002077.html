Yes, with the K2 (which I have) there is an annoying 10Hz or so max shift
when switching filters and you have to retune or click; the QSY button
actually helps deal with that, which is another reason I put it in.

I share Dick's goal of making sure that when hams set up fldigi for good
RX that they aren't lulled into bad TX.  And so I think that if you've set
up your rig's filters such that the peaks are at the same spot (+/- 10 Hz)
you're not going to get into a situation where your click off the sweet
spot and TX and get overdriven, since in general the filters will have
less sensitivity away from your chosen spot.

I'm glad you're having good luck with the SDR.  I have a few different
ones for RX but none of the Flex series, and am a contributor on quisk.
