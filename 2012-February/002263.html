The SCS company has developed an alternative mode to 300 baud Packet 
called Robust Packet. I've been testing it in one of their Tracker 
modems ($350) for some time now and it is robust. If you like Packet and 
need a modem that works under adverse conditions, this is it.

Short demo here http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZOLk4n1ypJU  The video 
was recorded during a test link between myself and N9LYA on the 20 meter 
band.

I'd love to see a similar mode in FLDIGI some day.

Robust Packet Specs:

Baud Rate: 200/600
Modulation: 8 tone PSK
Bandwidth: 500Hz

Tony, K2MO
