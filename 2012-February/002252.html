The problem is, "packet" desperately needs an overhaul. The use of an FSK
modem operating at 300 baud is seriously counterproductive on a HF path.
Packet needs a MUCH better modem with FEC. Likewise the use of fixed timing
in the protocol makes for poor performance in the presence of multiple
stations. Also packet could benefit through the adoption of selective
reject instead of go-back-N in the ARQ portion of the protocol.
