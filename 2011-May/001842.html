Mike,

Thanks for noting this -

This most likely has nothing to do with fldigi, and is a bug in Ubuntu 11.04.

Although it may not be a bug in the compiz package, I suggest you file
it there and if it's not compiz they can get it to the right place.

To open a bug against compiz in Ubuntu, type the following in a terminal:

ubuntu-bug compiz

This will gather information specific to your machine that may be
helpful in debugging the problem and open a bug report.
