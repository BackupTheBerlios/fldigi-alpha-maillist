A really great way to back up your log...and it's free.

http://www.dropbox.com/

If you are feeling charitable, if you use this link to sign up, they
give me more space for the referral.

http://db.tt/kIWZiV8

It's a 2GB free storage account that keeps files on the web.

You can get clients for linux, Windows Mac and for your Android and
likely other smart phones.  The online storage shows up as a 'folder'
on your system, making it very simple to use.  It also works like a
local directory for linux so ~/Dropbox as an example is where you can
just copy files you want to save.

Basically you set up multiple clients and they can all get to the same files.

You can also share out files to other people by setting the file
permissions and sharing links.

With 2GB, it's plenty of space for configuration files and things like
logbooks or other plain text files.

Just be sure to encrypt anything like a passwords collection.

This also shouldn't be your only copy in case you don't have
connectivity or the service ever goes down.
