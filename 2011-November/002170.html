Hi all,

Dave, A friend of mine (CO7VT) found a minor bug (without knowing it) in 
the pskreporter configuration of fldigi.. as for version 3.22.0BG, and I 
think that previous are affected... and it OS independent (my friend 
uses WinXP and I use Ubuntu)

At least in not connected to internet PC here in Cuba, when you tick 
some of the spotting (pskreporter) options in the configuration 
(activating the spotting feature) The software act as it was looking for 
connection to the internet upon restart, that's logical as the PC has no 
connection... and an error is raised about dns records not found... 
etc... this is normal...

But after you UN-tick all options on the spotter tab in Misc section of 
config (aka. disable this feature), and restart fldigi, the error about 
dns record still popping up on every restart of the program for now on...

I have made the "homework" the bug seems to be expressing on 
fldigi_def.xml file...

The option <USEPSKREP>0</USEPSKREP> is NOT returning to false on 
spotting deactivation...

And is causing the annoying error at start.

Try yourself...
