Yes, that is a successful use case, and thinking a little about it you 
can program a macro like this and you are done.

<TX>
<DTMF:12312312312312>            <- activation sequence
<RX>

The use of dtmf as a macro and not as a mode, let you insert the 
activation sequence independently of the mode being used, so it's a 
great feature.

Searching a little in google I discover that DTMF is 'not' a real 
communication mode in the full definition, due to his limited charset.

So Dave have make the homework, and I have learned a little more about 
the digital radio modes.

The DTMF TX as a macro is the right decision as I see it, so I correct 
myself about it.

But, I'm still wishing for the DTMF RX feature.... Like a "partial mode" 
as WWV and Frequency Analysis perhaps?
