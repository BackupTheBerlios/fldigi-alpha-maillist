I come with another wish/proposal... & waiting for the last one to 
become true, with my fingers crossed:

Reviewing many docs for a national movement for adopt by law some of the 
'old & new' digital modes[1]; I found that in 1996 Thomas Sailer 
(sailer at ife.ee.ethz.ch) make a soft called 'hf' for 
Amtor/Sitor/gtor/pactor/RTTY reception that's GPL v2, and his source 
code is freely available on the net (sorry about the link, digg in 
google: hf-0.1 thomas sailer amtor...)

Does anyone with programing skills give it a read & study for it's 
inclusion on fldigi as a new modems?

I know, it's alpha code but it's a half way ahead from start...

73.

[1]:Cuban hams are only allowed to do RTTY, AMTOR, SITOR, Clover, G-tor, 
Packet and PSK31; not MFSK, not Olivia, Not contestia... not PSK63, etc...

I'm running a nation wide movement for collect all the technical 
information of digital modes to present to the regulation autorities for 
review and approval. All this movement is supported by our national 
Radio Amateur Federation, and his debate place is a forum on our 
website, sorry private link for registered users only in www.frcuba.co.cu
