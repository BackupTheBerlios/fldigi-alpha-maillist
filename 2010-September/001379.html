Q15x25 mode uses fifteen PSK streams to return an effective 2500 baud data 
rate - if all goes well. In tests on HF, Q15x25 was not very robust at all. 
It would work well with a strong, clean link but under typical HF conditions 
it slowed down considerably. In some conditions, HF Packet at 300 baud 
provided better throughput than Q15x25 mode at 2500 baud.

As in the story of the tortoise and the hare, an emphasis on getting through 
can be much more productive than concentrating all effort upon simply going 
faster... Sometimes less is more.
