Interesting.

I am certainly no expert on DSP, but what about the idea of a scheme 
similar to MT63 with something like 12 channels of PSK250, each of which 
sending duplicate data in different time domains. This would put the 
total signal width well under 3.5k and would easily pass over standard 
FM transceivers and repeaters.

Then the other question would be what character coding would be best.

The problem, I would see, with running a straight PSK3000 signal would 
be that the slightest static crash at high speed would probably take out 
a few sentences. We put the limit on our psk operations with PSK250 for 
that reason.

-Dave, KB3FXI
