Bob,

It is only a matter of choosing the right font under Windows. There are 
thousands of fonts out there, and there must be one that matches the 
green machine font. Then you can use the "upper case" print option 
(coming soon) for mixed case modes.

My first RTTY machine was a Model 26 (paper tearer!) - always wished for 
a model 15 or 28, but computers came along and went that way.

BTW, there used to be a few font generators around so you can design 
your own font. Did that for DOS - not sure about Windows. I use fixedsys 
most of the time, as it is heavy and easy for me to read. Try Googling 
for TrueType font generators and something may come up.
