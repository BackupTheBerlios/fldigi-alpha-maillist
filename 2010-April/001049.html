Dave,

Thanks for the color suggestion.  Using the Plastic theme, I changed the 
color for SQL-1 and SQL-2 to another color, and the squelch check mark 
now shows up.  I think there may still be a bug because the squelch and 
AFC colors were set to the same before, and the AFC check mark showed up 
but not the squelch.  The squelch check mark would briefly appear (for 
maybe 1/4 second) then disappear.  However, this is no big deal, and not 
worth messing with I don't think since it is working fine now.  If you 
do want to fix it, perhaps the best thing to do is when the check mark 
option is selected, just force the color to black/white.

By the way, I prefer check marks over colors since I'm somewhat color 
blind and the check mark registers more easily to my mind when at a 
quick glance.  With colors, my brain has to spend time interpreting the 
color.  I suspect this is common to color-blind people, and may be even 
moreso to those who are more color blind than I am.  For that reason, I 
would like ALL the color buttons to have the check mark option.  
However, not too big a deal to me.
