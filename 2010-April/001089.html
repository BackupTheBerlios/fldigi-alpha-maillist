In the second line of controls, there is a widget that you can adjust for
the slant. I've got mine set to -.005 for this version and that results in a
straight image (that's on a Mac Mini, likely your value will be different.)
Slant, like manual centering, is a live option, works in just about an ideal
manner.

You will find that the autostarts, phasings and autostops lose reliability
with very weak and/or noisy signals, at which time manual operation is
called for. At that point, you use the centering control, which also works
in just about an ideal manner.

This is indeed an excellent implementation of WEFAX reception; it is coming
along beautifully.
