> Generally:
>
> 1) Tune your radio USB to 1.9 KHz below listed freq
> 2) Signal is from 1500 Hz (black) to 2300 Hz (white)
> 3) Narrow bandwidth on receiver if you can
> 4) There is a separate FAX reception window, see menu
>
> Signals on the frequencies are often also RTTY 850; same station, too. The
> FAX and RTTY alternates for some stations.
>
> Easy calculation: (a) subtract 2 KHz; (b) add .1 KHz.
>
> So 17147.4 = 17145.4, then *17145.5
