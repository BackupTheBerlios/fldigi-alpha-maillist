Well put Skip.

Our group is able to pass a tremendous amount of information with multiple
small bandwidth streams and reserve larger bandwidth modes for special needs
which are usually rare.

The problem arises when multiple stations attempt to pass traffic using wide
bandwidth modes.

We as Amateur Operators should use the least amount of bandwidth necessary
for effectiive communications at all times.

Perhaps that is why even MARS is exploring other digital modes and
encouraging our old standby CW.
